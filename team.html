About Spatial AI Lab

The Spatial AI Lab at Inner Mongolia University focuses on cutting-edge research in 3D Computer Vision, Spatial Intelligence, and Large Multi-modal Models. Our mission is to develop innovative algorithms and systems that enable machines to understand and interact with the 3D world.

Research Topics

3D Point Cloud Understanding
3D Perception & Scene Understanding
Large Multi-modal Models
Open-world Learning
View-based 3D Recognition
Practical AI Applications

Dr. Hongyu Sun leads the Spatial AI Lab, focusing on cutting-edge research in 3D computer vision and machine learning. With extensive experience in algorithm engineering and a strong academic background from Renmin University of China, Dr. Sun guides the team in developing innovative solutions for complex computer vision challenges.

His research has been published in top-tier venues including CVPR, NeurIPS, IEEE TVCG, and ICRA, with a focus on point cloud analysis, multi-view 3D understanding, and parameter-efficient learning methods. The laboratory welcomes motivated students and researchers interested in advancing the field of computer vision through collaborative research and practical applications.
