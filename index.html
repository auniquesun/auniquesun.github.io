About Me

I am a researcher at the College of Computer Science, Inner Mongolia University, where I lead the Computer Vision Research Laboratory. My research focuses on 3D Computer Vision, Machine Learning, and Algorithm Engineering, with particular interests in point cloud processing, multi-view 3D understanding, and efficient vision transformers.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science (2025) from Renmin University of China, supervised by Prof. Deying Li and Prof. Yongcai Wang. My academic journey includes a Master's degree from Renmin University (2017-2020) and a Bachelor's degree from Inner Mongolia University (2013-2017).
