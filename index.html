About Me

I am a researcher and faculty member at the College of Computer Science, Inner Mongolia University, China, where I lead the Spatial AI Lab. My research focuses on 3D Computer Vision, 3D Perception, and Large Multi-modal Models, with particular interests in point cloud processing, open-world learning, and practical AI systems.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science (2025) from Renmin University of China, supervised by Prof. Deying Li and Prof. Yongcai Wang. My academic journey includes a Master's degree from Renmin University (2017-2020) and a Bachelor's degree from Inner Mongolia University (2013-2017).
